Basic Components of an Off-Grid Solar System

Solar Panels

Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electrical energy using photovoltaic cells.

Batteries

Batteries store the electrical energy generated by solar panels for use when sunlight is not available.

Charge Controller

The charge controller regulates voltage and current from solar panels to protect batteries from overcharging.

Inverter

The inverter converts stored DC power from batteries into AC power usable by household appliances.
